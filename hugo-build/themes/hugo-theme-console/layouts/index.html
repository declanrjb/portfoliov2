> Declan Bradley is a reporting intern for The Chronicle of Higher Education and transfer student at Oberlin College. He is a graduate of the Lede Program for Data Journalism at Columbia University, and lives in Washington, D.C. In 2024, he was named Reporter of the Year by the Associated Collegiate Press. See his resume.

> His work, including visualizations of national campaign finance data and an investigation into the improper storage of student data at Reed College, has won top awards from the ACP in news writing, opinion, and multimedia journalism.

> His passion for data reporting has led him to volunteer as an analyst and scraper for both the Data Liberation Project and Open Elections, and he currently serves as a web developer for the Behind the Badge Project at MuckRock.
